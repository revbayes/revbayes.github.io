Bayesian Phylogenetics in RevBayes

Location: The Field Museum, Chicago, IL USA
Dates: 10/5, 2018
Instructors: Tracy Heath

Description

This workshop will cover basic phylogenetic inference in RevBayes for researchers affiliated with the Field Museum.

Location: Bill Stanley Classroom, 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

Software

Before the course starts, please install the following software on your own computer:
  • RevBayes v1.0.9
  • Tracer
  • FigTree
  • A simple text editor, such as Sublime Text, NotePad++, TextWrangler, BBEdit, vim, or emacs

Pre-workshop Materials

Please review the following materials before the workshop:
  • Paul Lewis's Primer on Phylogenetics Part 1 - Trees & Likelihood
  • Paul Lewis's Primer on Phylogenetics Part 2 - Substitution Models
  • Paul Lewis's Primer on Phylogenetics Part 3a - Bayesian Statistics & MCMC
  • Paul Lewis's Primer on Phylogenetics Part 3b - Bayesian Phylogenetics
